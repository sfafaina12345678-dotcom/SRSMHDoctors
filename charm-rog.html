Charm Rog Ki Pehchan: Kab Dermatologist Ko Dikhana Zaroori Hai?

Chamdi par khujli, daad, funsi ya daag-dhabbe ko log aksar mamuli samajh kar ghar ka ilaj karte rehte hain. Lekin kuch charm rog failne wale hote hain ya gambhir bimari ka sanket hote hain. Sahi samay par Dermatologist ko dikhana zaroori hai.

Dermatologist Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye? 8 Lakshan

1. 2 Hafte Se Zyada Khujli

Khujli 15 din se zyada hai aur tel-creme lagane se thik nahi ho rahi. Raat me khujli badh jati hai. Ye Scabies ya Fungal infection ho sakta hai.

2. Gol-Gol Laal Daag Jo Fail Rahe Ho

Chamdi par gol laal chakatte jinke kinare ubhre hue ho aur beech saaf ho. Ye Daad/Ringworm hai - ye ek se dusre ko failta hai.

3. Safed Daag Jo Badh Rahe Ho

Sharir par safed daag jo dheere-dheere badh rahe ho. Ye Vitiligo/Safed Daag ho sakta hai. Shuru me ilaj aasan hai.

4. Baal Guchche Me Jhadna

Kanghi karne par mutthi bhar baal nikal jaye ya sir me gol-gol jagah se baal gayab ho jaye. Ye Alopecia ka sanket hai.

5. Muhase Jo Thik Nahi Ho Rahe

Teenage ke baad bhi chehre, peeth par bade-bade daane, funsi aur gaddhe pad rahe ho. Cream se thik na ho.

6. Til Ka Rang-Roop Badalna

Purane til ka size, rang ya shape badal jaye. Khujli ya khoon nikle. Ye Skin Cancer ka sanket ho sakta hai.

7. Nakhoon Me Fungus

Pair ya haath ke nakhoon peele, mote aur bhur-bhure ho gaye ho. Aasani se toot jate ho.

8. Baar-Baar Phode-Funsi

Sharir par baar-baar phode-funsi nikle jo pak kar peep nikalti ho. Ye Sugar ya kamzori ka lakshan ho sakta hai.

Dermatologist Kya Ilaj Karte Hain?

Dermatologist chamdi, baal, nakhoon se judi sabhi bimari ka ilaj karte hain:

  • Daad, Khujli, Eczema, Psoriasis
  • Muhase, Daag-Dhabbe, Jhaiyaan
  • Baal Jhadna, Ganjiapan, Dandruff
  • Funsi, Phode, Skin Allergy
  • Til, Masse, Charm Cancer Ki Jaanch

Charm Rog Se Bachne Ke 6 Aasan Tips

  1. Roz Nahaye: Saaf paani se nahaye, khaas kar paseena aane ke baad.
  2. Dusre Ka Towel-Kanghi Na Use Kare: Daad-fungus failta hai.
  3. Cotton Ke Kapde Pehne: Synthetic kapde se garmi aur khujli hoti hai.
  4. Dhoop Se Bache: 11 AM - 3 PM tak tez dhoop me na nikle. Sunscreen lagaye.
  5. Steroid Cream Na Lagaye: Doctor ki salah ke bina cream lagana khatarnak hai.
  6. Paani Zyada Piye: 8-10 glass paani roz piye, chamdi healthy rahegi.

💡 Ghar Par Kya Na Kare?

  • Khujli wali jagah ko na khurche - infection badh jayega
  • Nimbu, lehsun, toothpaste jaise nuskhe na lagaye
  • Medical store se khud cream khareed kar na lagaye
