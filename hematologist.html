KhooN Ke Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye?

Thakan rehna, chakkar aana, baar-baar bukhar aana - isko log kamzori samajhkar taal dete hain. Lekin ye khoon ki bimari ke sanket bhi ho sakte hain. Is article me hum samjhenge ki kaunsi condition me Hematologist se consult karna chahiye.

| Hematologist Kya Dekhta Hai?

Hematologist yani Khoon ka specialist khoon, bone marrow aur lymph nodes se judi problem ka ilaj karte hain. Khoon ki kami, platelet kam, thalassemia, lymphoma, blood cancer jaisi problem ke liye inke paas jaya jata hai.

| Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye? 7 Lakshan

Agar ye symptoms dikhe to der na kare:

  • 1. Har Waqt Thakan: Poori neend ke baad bhi thakan, kamzori mehsoos hona.
  • 2. Peele Nakhoon/Peeli Aankh: Haath-pair ke nakhoon safed ya aankh peeli padna - khoon ki kami.
  • 3. Bina Chot Neel Padna: Jara sa lagne par sharir par neel pad jana ya chhote-chhote laal daane.
  • 4. Baar-Baar Bukhar: 1-2 hafte me bukhar aana jo antibiotic se bhi thik na ho.
  • 5. Bina Wajah Wajan Ghatna: 2-3 mahine me 5-6 kg wajan kam ho jana.
  • 6. Raat Me Pasina: Sote waqt itna pasina ki kapde bheeg jaye.
  • 7. Gaanth Mehsoos Hona: Gardan, bagal ya jangh me dard-rahit gaanth mehsoos hona.

| KhooN Ke Liye Zaroori Test

Test Ka Naam Kyun Karate Hain
CBC - Complete Blood Count Hemoglobin, WBC, Platelet count check karne ke liye - khoon ki kami pata chalti hai
Peripheral Smear Khoon ki cell ko microscope se dekhna - cancer cell ya kharab cell check karne ke liye
Bone Marrow Test Haddi ke andar khoon kaise ban raha hai - blood cancer confirm karne ke liye
