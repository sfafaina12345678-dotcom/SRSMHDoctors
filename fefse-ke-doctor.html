Fefde Ke Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye?

2 hafte se zyada khansi, saans phoolna, seene me se seeti bajna aaj kal aam ho gaya hai. Lekin har khansi ke liye cough syrup kaam nahi karta. Is article me hum samjhenge ki kaunsi condition me Pulmonologist se consult karna chahiye.

| Pulmonologist Kya Dekhta Hai?

Pulmonologist yani Chest Physician fefde, saans ki nali aur seene se judi problem ka ilaj karte hain. Dama, TB, COPD, nimonia, lambi khansi jaisi problem ke liye inke paas jaya jata hai.

| Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye? 7 Lakshan

Agar ye symptoms dikhe to der na kare:

  • 1. 2 Hafte Se Zyada Khansi: Agar khansi 15 din se zyada hai aur dawai se bhi thik nahi ho rahi.
  • 2. Saans Phoolna: Thoda chalne ya seedhi chadhne me saans chadh jaye.
  • 3. Seeti Ki Awaaz: Saans lete ya chhodte time seene se seeti bajna - Asthma ka sign.
  • 4. Balgam Me Khoon: Khansi ke saath balgam me khoon aana - TB ka khatra.
  • 5. Seene Me Dard: Khansne ya gahri saans lene par seene me chubhan ho.
  • 6. Raat Me Khansi Badhna: Sote time khansi se neend khul jaye.
  • 7. Wajan Kam Hona: Bina wajah wajan ghatna + khansi + bukhar aana.

| Fefde Ke Liye Zaroori Test

Test Ka Naam Kyun Karate Hain
Chest X-Ray Fefde me infection, TB, paani check karne ke liye
PFT - Pulmonary Function Test Fefda kitne % kaam kar raha hai, Asthma/COPD check karne ke liye
Sputum Test Balgam ki jaanch - TB ke bacteria dekhne ke liye
