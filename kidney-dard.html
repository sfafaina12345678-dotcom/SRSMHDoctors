Kidney Kharab Hone Par Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye?

Creatinine badhna, pair me sujan, peshab kam aana aaj kal aam ho gaya hai. Lekin har kidney problem ke liye turant dialysis nahi hota. Is article me hum samjhenge ki kaunsi condition me Nephrologist se consult karna chahiye.

| Nephrologist Kya Dekhta Hai?

Nephrologist kidney, BP aur sugar se judi problem ka ilaj karte hain. Kidney fail, high creatinine, dialysis, peshab me protein/jhaag, sujan jaisi problem ke liye inke paas jaya jata hai.

| Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye? 7 Lakshan

Agar ye symptoms dikhe to der na kare:

  • 1. Pair Aur Chehre Me Sujan: Subah uthte hi aankh ya pair foole hue lage.
  • 2. Peshab Me Jhaag Ya Khoon: Peshab karte time jhaag bane ya rang laal ho.
  • 3. Creatinine Badhna: Report me Creatinine 1.4 se zyada aaye.
  • 4. Peshab Kam Aana: Din me 1-2 baar hi peshab ho.
  • 5. Har Waqt Thakan: Bina kaam kiye bhi thakan lage, khoon ki kami ho.
  • 6. BP High Rehna: 3 dawai khane ke baad bhi BP 160/100 se upar rahe.
  • 7. Bhookh Na Lagna, Ulti Aana: Khana dekhte hi ulti jaisa lage.

| Kidney Ke Liye Zaroori Test

Test Ka Naam Kyun Karate Hain
KFT - Kidney Function Test Creatinine, Urea level check karne ke liye. Normal Creatinine 0.7-1.2 hota hai
Urine R/M Peshab me protein ya khoon hai ya nahi dekhne ke liye
Ultrasound KUB Kidney ka size chota-bada to nahi, sujan to nahi
