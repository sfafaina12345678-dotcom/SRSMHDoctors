Jabda-Chehre Ke Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye?

Jabda lock ho jana, muh na khulna, chehre ki haddi tootna aaj kal accident ke baad aam ho gaya hai. Lekin har daant ke dard ke liye dentist kaam nahi karta. Is article me hum samjhenge ki kaunsi condition me Maxillofacial Surgeon se consult karna chahiye.

| Maxillofacial Surgeon Kya Dekhta Hai?

Maxillofacial Surgeon yani Jabda-Chehra specialist muh, jabda, chehra, gardan aur khopdi ki haddi se judi problem ka operation se ilaaj karte hain. Jaw fracture, TMJ disorder, muh ka cancer, chehre ki chot jaisi problem ke liye inke paas jaya jata hai.

| Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye? 7 Lakshan

Agar ye symptoms dikhe to der na kare:

  • 1. Muh Poora Na Khulna: Khana khate waqt ya jamhai lete waqt muh 2 ungli se zyada na khul paaye.
  • 2. Jabda Lock Ho Jana: Baat karte-karte ya khaate waqt jabda atak jaye ya kat-kat ki awaz aaye.
  • 3. Chehre Par Sujan Ya Neel: Chot lagne ke baad chehra suj jaye, neela pad jaye ya chhune se tez dard ho.
  • 4. Daant Tedhe Ho Jana: Accident ke baad upar-neeche ke daant aapas me sahi se na mil rahe ho.
  • 5. Aankh Se Double Dikhna: Chehre ki chot ke baad aankh se do-do dikhe.
  • 6. Sunayi Kam Dena: Jabde ki chot ke baad kaan se sunayi kam de.
  • 7. Muh Me Lamba Chhala/Gaanth: Muh ke andar koi chhala ya gaanth 3 hafte se zyada ho gayi aur thik na ho rahi ho.
