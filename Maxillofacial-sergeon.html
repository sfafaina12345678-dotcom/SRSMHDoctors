Jabda Aur Chehre Ke Dard Me Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye?

Jabde me dard, chehre par chot ya muh kholne me dikkat aaj kal aam ho gayi hai. Lekin har dard ke liye doctor ke paas bhagna zaroori nahi hota. Is article me hum samjhenge ki kaunsi condition me Maxillofacial Surgeon se consult karna chahiye.

| Maxillofacial Surgeon Kya Dekhta Hai?

Maxillofacial Surgeon muh, jabda, chehra, gardan aur khopdi ki haddi se judi problem ka ilaj karte hain. Jabde ka fracture, daant ki surgery, muh na khulna, chehre ki chot, muh ke chhale jo thik na ho rahe ho, aur chehre ki haddi tedhi hone jaisi problem ke liye inke paas jaya jata hai.

| Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye? 7 Lakshan

Agar ye symptoms dikhe to der na kare:

  • 1. 2 Hafte Se Zyada Dard: Agar jabde ya chehre me dard 15 din se zyada hai aur painkiller se bhi thik nahi ho raha.
  • 2. Muh Poora Na Khulna: Khana khate waqt ya jamhai lete waqt muh 2 ungli se zyada na khul paaye.
  • 3. Chehre Par Sujan Ya Neel: Chot lagne ke baad chehra suj jaye, neela pad jaye ya chhune se tez dard ho.
  • 4. Jabda Lock Ho Jana: Baat karte-karte ya khaate waqt jabda atak jaye ya kat-kat ki awaz aaye.
  • 5. Daant Tedhe Ho Jana: Accident ke baad upar-neeche ke daant aapas me sahi se na mil rahe ho.
  • 6. Sunayi Ya Dikhayi Dene Me Dikkat: Chehre ki chot ke baad kaan se sunayi kam de ya aankh se double dikhe.
  • 7. Muh Me Lamba Chhala/Gaanth: Muh ke andar koi chhala ya gaanth 3 hafte se zyada ho gayi aur thik na ho rahi ho.
