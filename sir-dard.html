Sir Dard, Chakkar Aur Nerve Ki Problem: Kab Neuro Doctor Ko Dikhaye?

Sir dard ko log aksar tension ya neend ki kami samajh kar painkiller kha lete hain. Lekin kuch sir dard brain tumor, nas fatne ya nerve ki gambhir bimari ka sanket ho sakte hain. Sahi samay par Neurologist ya Neurosurgeon ko dikhana bahut zaroori hai.

⚠️ Emergency: Turant 108 Call Kare Agar:

  • Achanak bahut tez sir dard - "zindagi ka sabse tez dard"
  • Sir dard ke saath ulti aur gardan akad jaye
  • Ek taraf ka muh tedha ho jaye ya bol na paye
  • Haath-pair me achanak kamzori ya sunn ho jaye
  • Behoshi ya jhatke aaye - Mirgi ka daura
  • Chot ke baad sir dard aur ulti ya behoshi

Ye Brain Hemorrhage, Stroke ya Brain Injury ke lakshan hain. Har minute kimti hai.

Neuro Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye? 8 Lakshan

1. Lagatar 15 Din Se Zyada Sir Dard

Sir dard roz ho raha hai ya hafte me 3-4 din hota hai. Painkiller se thodi der aaram milta hai phir wapas aa jata hai. Ye Migraine ya Tension Headache ho sakta hai.

2. Subah Uthte Hi Sir Dard Aur Ulti

Subah aankh khulte hi sir me bhaari pan aur ulti jaisa lage. Ye Brain me pressure badhne ka sanket ho sakta hai.

3. Baar-Baar Chakkar Aana

Uthte-baithte ya gardan ghumane par chakkar aaye. Duniya ghoomti hui lage. Ye Vertigo ya Nerve ki problem ho sakti hai.

4. Haath-Pair Sunn Hona Ya Jhunjhuni

Haath ya pair me sui chubhne jaisi jhunjhuni, sunn pan ya jaldi thak jana. Cheez pakadne me gir jaye. Ye Nerve Dabne - Slip Disc ho sakta hai.

5. Yaad-Dasht Kamzor Hona

Chhoti-chhoti baatein bhool jana, raasta bhool jana, naam yaad na aana. 40-50 saal ki umar me ye Alzheimer/Dementia ka shuruwati lakshan ho sakta hai.

6. Neend Me Jhatke Aana Ya Mirgi

Neend me haath-pair jhatakna, muh se jhaag aana, jeebh kat jana. Hosh aane par kuch yaad na rahe. Ye Mirgi/Epilepsy hai.

7. Kamar Se Pair Tak Dard Jana

Kamar se hota hua dard pairo tak jaye. Chalne me pair bhaari lage ya sunn ho jaye. Ye Sciatica/Slip Disc ka lakshan hai.

8. Chehre Par Achanak Dard Ya Lakwa

Chehre ke ek taraf bijli ke current jaisa tez dard ya achanak muh tedha ho jana. Aankh band na ho. Ye Trigeminal Neuralgia ya Facial Palsy hai.

Neurologist vs Neurosurgeon - Kise Dikhaye?

Neurologist: Dawa se ilaj karte hain. Migraine, Mirgi, Chakkar, Slip Disc, Stroke ka pehla ilaj ye karte hain. CT Scan, MRI, EEG jaanch karate hain.

Neurosurgeon: Operation karte hain. Brain tumor, nas fatna, slip disc ka operation, head injury ka ilaj ye karte hain.

Pehle Neurologist ko dikhaye. Agar operation ki zaroorat hui to wo Neurosurgeon ke paas refer kar denge.

Brain Aur Nerve Ko Healthy Kaise Rakhe? 6 Tips

  1. BP-Sugar Control: High BP brain ki nase phad sakta hai - Stroke ka sabse bada karan.
  2. 7-8 Ghante Neend: Kam neend se sir dard aur mirgi badhti hai.
  3. Helmet Zaroori: Bike chalate time helmet head injury se bachata hai.
  4. Smoking-Sharab Band: Ye brain ki nase kamzor karti hai.
  5. Mobile/Gardan Ka Angle: Gardan jhuka ke mobile na dekhe - Cervical problem hoti hai.
  6. Stress Kam Kare: Meditation, walk se tension sir dard kam hota hai.

  • Thanda paani ya barf ka tukda maathe par rakhe
  • Adrak wali chai ya coffee pi sakte hain
  • Painkiller hafte me 2 baar se zyada na khaye
