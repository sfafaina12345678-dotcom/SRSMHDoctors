Dil Ki Bimari Ke Shuruwati Lakshan: Kab Cardiologist Ko Dikhana Zaroori Hai?

Seene me dard ya bhaari pan ko log aksar gas ya acidity samajh ke ignore kar dete hain. Lekin kabhi-kabhi ye dil ki gambhir bimari ka sanket ho sakta hai. Sahi samay par Cardiologist ko dikhana jaan bacha sakta hai.

⚠️ Emergency: Turant 108/102 Call Kare Agar:

  • Seene me tez dard jo baye haath, gardan ya jabde me ja raha ho
  • Achanak saans bahut phoolna bina mehnat ke
  • Thanda paseena aur chakkar ke saath ulti jaisa lage
  • Behoshi ya gir padna

Ye heart attack ke lakshan ho sakte hain. 1 minute ki bhi deri khatarnak hai.

Cardiologist Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye? 7 Lakshan

1. Seene Me Baar-Baar Bhaari Pan Ya Dard

Chalne ya sidhi chadhne par seene me dabaav, jalan ya bhaari pan lage jo aram karne se thik ho jaye. Ye Angina ho sakta hai.

2. Bina Wajah Saans Phoolna

Thoda sa chalne par ya raat me sote time saans phool jaye. Takiya laga ke sona pade. Ye dil ke kamzor hone ka sanket hai.

3. Pair Aur Takhno Me Sujan

Shaam tak pair me sujan aa jaye jo dabaane se gaddha ban jaye. Ye Heart Failure ka lakshan ho sakta hai.

4. Bahut Tez Ya Aniyamit Dhadkan

Dil achanak bahut tez dhadkne lage ya dhadkan chhoot jaye. Seene me phadphadahat mehsoos ho. Ye Arrhythmia kehlata hai.

5. Baar-Baar Chakkar Ya Behoshi

Uthte-baithte chakkar aaye ya aankhon ke aage andhera chha jaye. Kabhi behosh ho kar gir pade.

6. Lagatar Khansi Jisme Safed Jhag Nikle

Letne par khansi badh jaye aur balgum me gulaabi jhag aaye. Ye fefdo me paani bharne ka sanket hai.

7. Family History + BP/Sugar

Agar parivar me kisi ko 50 saal se pehle heart attack hua ho, aur aapko BP, Sugar ya Cholesterol hai to regular jaanch zaroori hai.

Cardiologist Kya Jaach Karte Hain?

Cardiologist dil aur khoon ki nase se judi bimari ka ilaj karte hain. Ye jaanch karte hain:

  • ECG: Dil ki dhadkan ka graph
  • Echo: Dil ka ultrasound
  • TMT: Chalke dil ki jaanch
  • Angiography: Nase me blockage dekhna

Dil Ko Healthy Kaise Rakhe? 5 Aasan Tips

  1. Namak Kam Khaye: Din me 1 chammach se kam. Achar, papad, namkeen se bache.
  2. 30 Minute Tez Chale: Roz subah ya shaam tez chalna dil ko mazboot karta hai.
  3. Smoking Bilkul Band: Bidi-cigarette dil ki nase band kar deti hai.
  4. Vajan Control: Motaapa dil ka sabse bada dushman hai.
  5. BP-Sugar Check: 30 saal ke baad har 6 mahine me jaanch karaye.
