Haddi Aur Jod Ke Dard Me Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye?

Ghutne, kamar, gardan ya kandhe me dard aaj kal aam ho gaya hai. Lekin har dard ke liye doctor ke paas bhagna zaroori nahi hota. Is article me hum samjhenge ki kaunsi condition me Ortho Surgeon se consult karna chahiye.

Ortho Surgeon Kya Dekhta Hai?

Ortho Surgeon haddi, jod, ligament aur muscle se judi problem ka ilaj karte hain. Fracture, gathiya, slip disc, kamar dard jaisi problem ke liye inke paas jaya jata hai.

Doctor Ko Kab Dikhana Chahiye? 7 Lakshan

Agar ye symptoms dikhe to der na kare:

  • 1. 2 Hafte Se Zyada Dard: Agar dard 15 din se zyada hai aur aram se bhi thik nahi ho raha.
  • 2. Sujan Aur Laalpan: Jod ke aas-paas sujan aa jaye ya chhune se garam lage.
  • 3. Jod Jaam Ho Jana: Subah uthne par ghutna ya ungli seedhi na ho.
  • 4. Chalne Me Dikkakat: Seedhi chadhne-utrne me dard ho.
  • 5. Chot Lagna: Girne ke baad tez dard, neela padna.
  • 6. Raat Me Dard Badhna: Sote time dard se neend khul jaye.
  • 7. Sunn Hona: Haath ya pair me jhanjhanahat ya sunnpan aaye.
